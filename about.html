About Our Project

FASTag is an electronic toll collection system in India, operated by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). It employs Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid or savings account linked to it or directly toll owner. A smart toll gate system using RFID technology automates toll collection by reading a unique Radio Frequency Identification tag attached to a vehicle's windshield, allowing for seamless passage through toll plazas without stopping to pay cash, significantly reducing traffic congestion and improving efficiency; the system deducts the toll amount directly from a prepaid account linked to the RFID tag, providing a convenient and fast payment method.

In seach of INCREDIBLE!

We are making toll systems smart to reduce traffic congestion and minimize delays at toll plazas. Smart toll systems ensure seamless and cashless transactions, enhancing convenience for travelers. They help save fuel and time while promoting eco-friendly transportation. Additionally, they improve accuracy, transparency, and security in toll collection processes.
